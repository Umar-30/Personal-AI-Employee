# Business Goals

## Strategic Objectives

- Reduce cost per task through AI automation.
- Increase consistency of business operations.
- Maintain executive-level quality in all outputs.

## Key Metrics

- Tasks completed per week
- Average task completion time
- Approval turnaround time
- Error rate (tasks requiring rework)

## Current Priorities

1. Operational efficiency — automate repetitive tasks
2. Communication management — email and social media
3. Financial oversight — invoicing and reporting
4. Strategic planning — weekly briefings and analysis
